THE LINE [Saudi Arabia]

❮ ❯

The line was my latest project with screenworks productions. I worked on bringing life to the model by creating new texures and materials, creating my own custom props such as the holograms, and building and programming the trains in the video. Like the other projects I worked on, lighting the scene correctly was essential to the project.

I really enjoyed this project, partly because of the high stakes and high pressure development environment I was forced to work under. This project was given on extremely late notice, such that we had exactly 1 week to produce a finalised render of this project.

LOT3 MALL [Bangkok]

❮ ❯
In this project, I was tasked with working on the interior section of the showcase. Populating the space will realistic and plausible props such as restaurants, shops, and furniture at this scale was a difficult feat. If done incorrectly, the entire project would look like a unprofessional asset flip.

I also had diffuclties with creating realistic interior lighting. Since most of the interior is blocked from natural sunlight, filling in dark spots was difficult, especially with the amount of empty rooms.

THANE [India]

❮ ❯
This project is by far my favourite. I worked on both the exterioir and interior sections. The requirements were quite loose, so I was able to really let myself become the designer and add my own constructions, scenes, and models.

For instance, the timelapse pool shot, the trains, the highway animations, and the LED screens were all design choices made by solely by me and my coworkers.

This time we tried to push the animation side of the project to bring more life to it. I feel this really payed off and enabled the model to seem like it really could be a building in Thane.

HARBOUR LOOP [Hong Kong]

❮ ❯
This was my very first project that I collaborated on during my first internship in 2020. This project is also the first time I had ever tried doing anything in the digital media industry.

I mainly focused mostly on making custom models. The theme for this project was to emulate a possible near future. Thus, I was tasked with creating models of futuristic models in 3DS MAX and naturally integrating them into the architectural model.

You can see my work in some of the shots, the pier, the cinema boat, and the water taxi's were all my creations.
